Alex's Marinara Recipe

Recipe Description

This is a simple homemade sauce that tastes 100x better than any store bought canned sauce. Use at you own discretion, you wil never go back!

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves chopped Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fresh Basil
  • 4 Roma Tomatoes
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • 2 Cans of Tomato Paste

Recipe Steps

  1. Put chopped tomatoes, basil, garlic, italian seasoning, and olive oil into a food processor
  2. Add mixture to a sauce pan on medium
  3. Cook for 5 minutes, then add cayenne to taste
  4. Add 2 cans of tomato paste to thicken sauce
  5. Serve!
